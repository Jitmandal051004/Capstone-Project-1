THE BEST ANIME ACCORDING TO INDRAJIT

My Top 3 Anime of all-time.

  1. Kimi no na wa

    Your Name is a visually stunning anime movie about two high school students who mysteriously swap bodies.
    As they navigate each other's lives, they discover a deeper connection and a looming disaster that threatens
    their town. With its captivating story and stunning animation, Your Name has become a beloved classic in the
    anime world.

  2. Vinland Saga

    Vinland Saga is an epic historical anime and manga series that takes place in medieval Scandinavia.
    The story follows the journey of Thorfinn, a young warrior seeking revenge for his father's death.
    Along the way, he gets caught up in a brutal Viking conflict and historical events that shape the
    world around him. With its intense action scenes, compelling characters, and intricate historical details, Vinland Saga
    is a thrilling and thought-provoking adventure that will keep you on the edge of your seat. It has gained
    a passionate following worldwide, and for good reason.

  3. Demon Slayer

    Demon Slayer is an action-packed and visually stunning anime and manga series that takes
    you on a thrilling adventure through a fantasy version of Japan. Follow the determined protagonist,
    Tanjiro Kamado, and his lovable companions as they hunt down demons and face incredible challenges.
    With its engaging storyline, beautiful animation, and memorable characters, Demon Slayer has captured
    the hearts of fans worldwide.
